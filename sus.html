Sustainability at YSL Builders

Environmental consciousness is at the heart of our operations. We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint across all our projects while maintaining our reputation for delivering high-quality reliable services. Sustainability is woven into every aspect of our business ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. By partnering closely with our clients, we ensure that our methods are both efficient and environmentally friendly.

Environmental Stewardship

Our dedication to environmental stewardship drives us to minimize our impact on air, water, land, and communities while enhancing biodiversity and preserving cultural heritage. We have implemented an Environmental Management System certified to ISO 14001:2015, which guides us in assessing all our projects with a focus on reducing, reusing, and recycling to minimize waste.

Resource Efficiency

We prioritize the sustainable use of natural resources and advocate for the transition to a circular economy. This means conserving water, reducing waste through smart design, and maximizing the reuse and recovery of materials. We utilize recycled and secondary materials from other industries, making them integral to our products and fuels. Remarkably, we consume more waste than we produce, underscoring our commitment to resource efficiency.

Recycling and Use of Recycled Materials

Our approach to waste management is proactive and innovative, with a strong emphasis on recycling and reusing. Sending waste to landfill is a last resort; instead, we aim to recycle 98% of all waste generated. In our concrete mixing and aggregate production, we use top-quality recycled materials from our own sites. Our asphalt production adheres to the Waste and Resources Action Programme (WRAP) standards, ensuring minimal environmental impact. We collaborate with our contacts and contracts to repurpose materials, and when reuse is not possible, we segregate and recycle appropriately.

Climate Change and Energy Management

Recognizing our industryâ€™s significant impact on natural resources, we take a holistic approach to managing energy and greenhouse gas emissions throughout the lifecycle of our operations. This encompasses material selection, operational processes, product transportation, performance during use, and end-of-life recycling or disposal. Our investments in energy-efficient technologies, waste-derived fuels, renewable energy sources, and responsible supply chain management are pivotal in reducing our carbon footprint.

Sustainable Procurement

Adhering to a "Sustainable Green Procurement Policy" and the Mayor of Londonâ€™s code for Green Procurement, we prioritize purchasing recyclable and environmentally friendly products that meet required standards and are financially viable. Our staff is guided by Sustainable Green Procurement principles to ensure sustainable purchasing decisions.

Fleet & Transport

To mitigate pollutant emissions such as Nitrous Oxides (NOx) and Particulate Matter (PM10), our entire diesel fleet is equipped with Adblue, an additive that reduces emissions through selective catalytic reduction. All our Light Goods Vehicles (LGVs) and plant equipment operate on Ultra Low Sulphur Diesel (ULSD).

Visual Impact

We are committed to minimizing the visual impact of our construction activities on the local environment. By limiting on-site storage of construction materials and transporting them only as needed, we strive to maintain a clean and unobtrusive worksite.

Public Impact

Transparency and communication are key to our operations. We keep local residents and businesses informed about our work programs, providing advance notifications of upcoming activities and explaining the measures we take to minimize disruptions.

Our Commitment to a Greener Tomorrow

At YSL Builders Ltd, we are passionate about fostering a greener tomorrow through responsible environmental practices. By integrating sustainability into every facet of our operations, we not only protect our planet but also ensure the longevity and success of our business and the communities we serve.
